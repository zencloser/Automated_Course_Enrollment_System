Frequently asked questions
How do I enroll in a course?▼
Go to the Enrollment section in the sidebar. Select your name and the course you wish to join, then click "Enroll / join waitlist". If the course is full, you will be automatically added to the waitlist.
What happens when my attendance drops below 75%?▼
An automatic alert is generated by the database trigger trg_attendance_warning and sent to the Alerts panel. You may be barred from exams if attendance remains below 75% at the end of the semester.
How is my CGPA calculated?▼
CGPA is computed using the credit-weighted formula: Sum of (Grade/10 × Credits) divided by Total Credits, across all enrolled courses. Use the GPA Calculator tool in the sidebar to estimate your predicted CGPA.
When are certificates issued?▼
Certificates are auto-issued by the trg_certificate_issue trigger when your completion percentage reaches 100% and your grade is 40 or above. You can find your certificates in the Grades section.
How can I pay my course fees in installments?▼
Visit the Payments section, select your name, and click "View" against any course to see the installment schedule. Contact the Fee Department at fees@snu.edu.in if you need to set up a new installment plan.
How does the waitlist work?▼
When a course is full, you are placed on the waitlist. When any enrolled student drops the course, the trg_waitlist_promote trigger automatically promotes the highest-priority student (senior students first, then by position).
What scholarship tiers are available?▼
Scholarships are awarded based on CGPA: Gold (9.0+), Silver (8.0–8.9), Bronze (7.0–7.9). Results are computed live using the fn_scholarship_tier database function. Visit the Reports section to see all eligible students.
